May 3rd, 2015 - Values and Combatting Stereotype Threat

When I have felt the happiest, the following values are what comes to mind.

  • Tranquility
  • Nature
  • Friendships
  • Creativity
  • Accomplishment
  • Meaningful work

On a scale of one to five, I think I try to live up to these values five times out of five. For example, meaningful work is the entire reason I am moving to Chicago and attending dev bootcamp. Coding is creative, it matter in society today, and it requires a degree of focus and hardwork that I find extremely rewarding.

I feel affirmed when I think about my values. I think if I ever encountered stereotype threat, I would be able to lean on my values as affirmation that I am not the sum total of what others think of me. Rather, I am largely what my values dictate. I think if I am feeling down at DBC, the two things I will do the most are meditate and talk to others. I have found that seeking inner peace and sharing my feelings with others go the longest way towards making me feel better.
